Membership

The Rutgers Astronomical Society is a club that does not require a fee from any of its members or visitors for our events.

Visitors/Public

Visitors are those that wish only to attend our public observing nights and other open public events. Visitors are free to bring their own equipment out to the observing nights, however please be careful to collect all your belongings afterwards. We also ask visitors respect the room and rooftop during these events. Members

A member in our club wishes to do more with the club trips and professor talks targetted towards said members so We hope to provide members additional opportunities such as participating in student organized research, our astrophotography team, etc.Members are expected to help with setting up for observing nights, talking with visitors, and learning telescope operations (this includes the usage of our .5 m telescope in the dome). To be a member you must

  • Be a currently attending Rutgers student
  • Have gone to three of our events with no more than two weeks between each visit/sign in.
  • Have A passion for astronomy!
  • Be in good standing
  • Good at working with others
